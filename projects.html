Professional Projects


31 Days To Die

This is a project that we did in college, where we were put into groups and made games for the Godot Wild Jam #62. I mainly focused on the programming side of the game. It is a Doom-like wave survival game. I am very happy with how it turned out. The story of the game is that you play as a living apple and you need to protect your boss who lives in a giant pumpkin while being attacked by giant squirrels and you only weapon is throwing apples at them (Until layer where you unlock anoh another weapon but you have to play it to find out what it is). Overall the game came out pretty good there are a few things that we could improve like variety of enemies but as we are still learning I think we did a really good job.
