About Me


Hi my name is Jakub Burchart. I am 17 years old and I was born in Poland. I have been playing games since I was very young and for a long time I have been interested in how games are made and how they work especially games with interesting features such as Portal 1 and 2 and Superliminal, No Time and Viewfinder.

I am mainly interested in the programming side of game delvelopment but I do also like the design side (making textures/sprites, level design, etc.) I have knowledge in using C# and GDscript and also some other programming languages such as HTML, CSS, Python and a bit of Javascript.

I am currently a student doing a Level 3 games course at City of Bristol College.
